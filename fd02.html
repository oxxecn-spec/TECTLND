Perfect Folding Screen Door for High‑Traffic & Pet‑Friendly Homes

Two panel options to match your lifestyle – robust mesh or transparent PVC

🐕 Pet‑Friendly Homes: 304 Stainless Steel Dot Mesh

If you have dogs, cats, or other pets that love to scratch or push against doors, the 304 stainless steel dot mesh is your best defence. Unlike flimsy fabric screens that tear within weeks, the FD02’s metal mesh is virtually indestructible. The round‑dot pattern provides excellent visibility and airflow while blocking even the smallest insects. The frame and tracks are also reinforced to withstand repeated sliding and pet impacts. This makes the FD02 perfect for garage door mosquito nets, back entrances, and outdoor workshops where pets come and go frequently.

Many customers in Australia and the USA have installed the FD02 on their patio doors – their dogs can lean against the screen without damaging it, and cats cannot shred the metal. Cleaning is simple: a quick hose or wipe and the mesh looks like new.

🍳 Kitchens & Cooking Areas: Crystal Clear PVC Panels

For kitchens, the crystal clear PVC folding panels are the superior choice. The transparent material lets natural light flood into the space while creating a barrier against flying insects, cooking smells (if you want to contain them), and dust. Unlike mesh, PVC is grease‑resistant and easy to wipe clean with a damp cloth – no scrubbing required. The folding design allows you to open the entire doorway when moving between kitchen and dining area, then close it again in seconds.

Restaurants and cafes have also adopted the FD02 with PVC panels for their pass‑through windows and open kitchens. The crystal‑clear surface maintains hygiene standards while allowing customers to see the food preparation area. In Europe and North America, the FD02 is becoming a popular kitchen fly screen door solution for both residential and commercial properties.

🚪 Garage Doors & Workshops

Garages are often neglected when it comes to insect protection, but they are major entry points for mosquitoes, flies, and wasps – especially if you leave the main door open for ventilation. The FD02 mounts directly inside the garage door frame. The 304 stainless steel mesh option withstands accidental car bumps, tools, and pet scratches. The concertina design takes up minimal space on the side, unlike sliding or hinged screens that require clearance.

Workshops storing wood, paint, or food products also benefit from the FD02. The fine mesh keeps out dust and insects, while the PVC option provides a totally cleanable surface for areas with airborne particles. For garages in hot climates, the mesh version promotes natural cross‑ventilation without letting bugs in.

🏡 Living Rooms & Villa Gates

The FD02 is equally at home in elegant living rooms and grand villa entrances. The wood grain frame finish matches wooden double doors, and the crystal clear PVC panels create an almost invisible barrier – you hardly notice the screen is there. When you want an open plan feel, the bi‑parting system allows both sides to fold away completely. Unlike fixed screens, the FD02 preserves your interior design while providing essential mosquito protection.

For villa entry screens in tropical countries (Thailand, Malaysia, Florida), the 304 mesh option withstands high humidity and salt air without rusting. The aluminium frame is also powder‑coated to resist corrosion. Many villa developers are now specifying FD02 units for new builds because of their durability and low maintenance.

🏨 Commercial & Hospitality Applications

Beyond homes, the FD02 is used in hotels (staff entrances, service doors), restaurants (kitchen pass‑throughs), and shops (doorway insect barriers while keeping display visibility). The crystal clear PVC version is especially popular for storefronts – customers can see the products inside even when the screen is closed. The concertina folding system is quiet and does not scare away nervous customers.
