Perfect Folding Screen Door for Every Large Opening

From Mediterranean villas to Australian patios – MBORMC FD05 delivers robust protection

🏛️ Villa Main Entrances & Courtyard Gates

For villa owners, the FD05 is the ideal villa entry door screen. Its wide‑span design (up to 4 metres) covers double‑door entrances without needing a central post. The optional wood grain finish complements natural stone or wooden façades, while the 304 stainless steel concertina mesh withstands coastal humidity, pet claws, and accidental kicks. When fully open, the mesh folds into a slim stack (approx. 15cm per leaf), leaving your entrance completely unobstructed.

Many of our clients use the FD05 as a courtyard gate screen – mounted directly onto existing iron or wooden gates. The sliding mechanism is smooth and silent, and the compact folding ensures the screen does not protrude into walkways. For large villas in Spain, Italy, Greece, and the Middle East, the FD05 has become a preferred choice.

🍃 Patio Doors & Garden Entrances

Patio doors with wide glass panels are often left open for airflow, but flies and mosquitoes quickly spoil the experience. The FD05 provides an invisible barrier. The bi‑parting (French door) configuration allows both panels to slide from the centre outwards, meeting in the middle or folding to opposite sides. This is perfect for French doors that open outward – the screen tracks mount inside the frame without interfering with the door handles.

Homeowners in the UK, USA, and Canada appreciate the white or grey aluminium frame that matches their uPVC windows. The frame is powder‑coated to resist scratches and UV fading. Because the FD05 is made‑to‑measure, we can fit any non‑standard arch or trapezoid shape (contact us for custom shapes).

🏡 Residential Main Doors & Apartment Entrances

For houses and ground‑floor apartments, the front door is often a major entry point for insects. The FD05 can be installed as a single‑leaf folding screen on the inner side of the main door. The 304 stainless steel mesh is fine enough to block midges and mosquitoes while allowing 85% airflow and outward visibility. The foldable design means you can push the screen completely aside when carrying groceries or furniture; it will not become a permanent obstacle.

Unlike retractable screens that roll up into a cassette, the FD05’s concertina mesh is self‑cleaning (dust and pollen fall through) and never jams. The robust aluminium frame also provides a solid feel – no bending or wobbling.

🏨 Hotels, Resorts & Commercial Spaces

Hospitality businesses love the FD05 for restaurant patio doors, conference room entrances, and poolside cabanas. The large span (up to 4 metres) covers double‑door exits to terraces. The stainless steel mesh is easy to wipe clean and resists repeated sliding cycles (tested > 50,000 operations). For hotels in Southeast Asia, the Caribbean, and Mediterranean, the FD05 provides a seamless blend of insect protection and open‑air dining.
