Perfect Retractable Screen Door for Every Door Opening

Cool your home naturally – insect protection meets solar shielding

🏝️ Villas & Luxury Homes in Tropical Climates

For villas in Florida, Thailand, the Caribbean, and Australia, the H03 is the ideal villa entry screen. The high‑density PET mesh reduces the greenhouse effect commonly experienced on west‑facing patios and glass doors. By reflecting up to 90% of UV radiation, the H03 keeps your living room cooler under the midday sun, reducing air‑conditioning costs. The retractable design means you can roll the screen away completely during cooler months, preserving your unobstructed view of gardens or pools.

The bi‑parting (French door) configuration is particularly popular for double‑door entrances. Each side operates independently – you can open one leaf for quick access or both for a full opening. The top and bottom guide rails are discrete and do not interfere with door handles or locks.

🍹 Patio Doors, Conservatories & Sunrooms

Patio doors are the main entry point for flies and mosquitoes, but traditional fixed screens block the view and feel claustrophobic. The H03 solves this with its roll‑up mechanism – when not needed, the screen completely retracts into a 90mm cassette, leaving your glass door fully exposed. The PET mesh has an 85%+ transparency, so even when the screen is down, you still enjoy a bright, open feel.

For conservatories and sunrooms, the H03’s solar reflective mesh is a game‑changer. It reduces glare and keeps the room comfortable without dark curtains. The material is also resistant to mildew and salt air – ideal for coastal homes. Many homeowners in the UK, Ireland, and Scandinavia use the H03 to keep their glass‑enclosed spaces insect‑free while enjoying natural light.

🚪 Room Entrances & Commercial Spaces

The H03 is also an excellent choice for internal room entrances – for example, connecting a living room to a balcony or a kitchen to a sun terrace. In restaurants and cafes, the retractable screen door allows staff to keep doors open for service while blocking flies from the dining area. The PET mesh is easy to wipe clean and does not rust, even with frequent use.

For office buildings with glass entrance doors, the H03 provides a professional, invisible insect barrier. The aluminium frame can be powder‑coated to match corporate colours. Because it is made‑to‑measure, we can fit any non‑standard opening up to 3 metres wide.

🏨 Hotels, Resorts & Serviced Apartments

Hoteliers love the H03 for ground‑floor rooms and poolside cabanas. The solar‑shield PET fiber keeps guest rooms cooler, reducing the load on HVAC systems – a real cost saving in large properties. The retractable design is also guest‑friendly: no heavy sliding or noisy tracks. We provide OEM branding options, including laser‑engraved logos on the cassette and custom packaging.

Popular installations include resort villas in the Maldives, beachfront hotels in Mexico, and boutique hotels in Italy. The H03’s rust‑proof mesh withstands salty sea breezes, and the 2‑year warranty gives property managers peace of mind.

🌎 Global Compatibility & Ordering Process

The H03 works with most door frame materials – wood, aluminium, uPVC, steel, and stone. The cassette mounts to the side of the door frame (left or right), and top/bottom tracks are fixed with screws. For a perfect installation, you will need a spirit level, drill, measuring tape, and a screwdriver. We provide a detailed PDF manual and video tutorials in English, French, German, and Spanish. A confident DIYer can complete the installation in 2‑3 hours; otherwise, we recommend a local handyman.

Each H03 is made‑to‑order at our MBORMC factory in Dali Town, Nanhai, Foshan. After production (typically 15‑20 days), the unit is carefully packed in a reinforced wooden crate to prevent damage during sea freight (30‑45 days) or air freight (5‑10 days). We have shipped to the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, France, Germany, Netherlands, Sweden, Singapore, Malaysia, and the UAE.
