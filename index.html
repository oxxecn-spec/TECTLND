About MBORMC

Engineering Excellence from Foshan, China – Delivered Worldwide

MBORMC is a professional manufacturer with 15+ years of aluminium extrusion and precision engineering, specializing in retractable insect screens, folding door systems, and window mesh solutions. Serving over 50 countries, our products are trusted by global importers, hardware retailers, and construction firms who demand high quality & factory direct pricing.

All products are built with premium 6063‑T5 aluminum alloy – offering superior corrosion resistance and durability. We provide full OEM/ODM services from frame color, mesh type to custom packaging & logo printing.
