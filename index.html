About MBORMC

Engineering Excellence from Foshan, China – Delivered Worldwide

MBORMC is a professional manufacturer of high‑quality retractable insect screens, folding door systems, and window mesh solutions. With over 15 years of aluminium extrusion and precision engineering experience, we serve residential, hotel, and commercial projects in more than 50 countries. Our products are trusted by global importers, DIY retailers, and construction companies.

All our products are manufactured in our Foshan facility using premium 6063‑T5 aluminium alloy – offering high strength, corrosion resistance, and a beautiful finish. We combine factory‑direct pricing with global logistics and OEM/ODM capabilities to meet any client requirement.
