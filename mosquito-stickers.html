Perfect No‑Drill Fly Screen for Rentals, Pet Owners & Every Home

Strong adhesion, clean removal, and serious cat‑proof durability

🏢 Apartments & Rental Properties

Landlords often prohibit drilling into window frames – and tenants don't want to risk losing their deposit. The magic tape mosquito net solves both problems. The self‑adhesive tape sticks securely to aluminium, uPVC, wood, and metal frames, yet removes cleanly without leaving sticky residue or damaging the paint. When you move out, simply peel off the tape and the window is as good as new.

Thousands of tenants across the UK, USA, Australia, and Canada have installed our self adhesive window screen within minutes – no tools, no handyman, no arguments with landlords. The white border blends with white uPVC frames, and the grey mesh looks clean and modern.

🐱 Cat Owners & Pet‑Friendly Homes

Traditional fibreglass mesh frays and tears when cats scratch or climb. Our polyester fiber mesh is much more resilient. It resists snagging, does not develop pulled threads, and bounces back from paw impacts. The tight weave also prevents claws from getting caught. Cat owners report that their pets eventually lose interest because the screen doesn't give them the satisfying "scratch" sensation.

For dog owners, the mesh stands up to enthusiastic nose pokes and occasional pawing. The hook‑and‑loop attachment even allows the screen to pop open slightly under strong impact (reducing the chance of tearing), then snaps back into place.

🏡 Homeowners with Outward‑Opening or Non‑Standard Windows

Many homes have windows that open outward (casement, awning, or tilt‑and‑turn). Rigid frame screens often block the opening or require complicated brackets. The no drill insect screen mounts directly on the interior frame and does not interfere with the window operation. Because the mesh is flexible, you can still reach through to close or open the window handle. The low profile of the Velcro tape (only 2mm thick) means the screen sits flush and does not obstruct your view.

For unusually shaped or large windows, we custom‑make each screen to your exact measurements. The magic tape system handles widths up to 2000mm with no sagging – no central support needed.

🪟 Bedrooms, Living Rooms & Kitchens

The fine mesh (18×16 threads per inch) blocks mosquitoes, midges, and even sand flies – ideal for bedrooms where a single mosquito can ruin a night's sleep. In kitchens, it keeps flies away from food while allowing steam and cooking smells to escape. The white border gives a tidy, finished appearance that complements any interior style. The grey mesh is less visible than black or dark green, so it doesn't dominate the window.

Unlike fixed screens that require professional installation, the removable fly screen can be taken down seasonally. During winter, simply roll the screen and store it; in spring, re‑attach it in seconds. The adhesive tape will last for seasons if kept clean.

🏨 Hotels, Guesthouses & Short‑Term Rentals

Properties that host international guests increasingly install damage‑free insect screens. Tourists often leave windows open for fresh air, inviting mosquitoes inside. The Magic Tape screen provides an instant, invisible barrier. Because it leaves no marks, hotel owners can install them in all rooms without worrying about future renovations. The cat‑proof mesh also stands up to accidental luggage bumps and cleaning staff wiping the screens.

For Airbnb hosts, the screen is a highly appreciated amenity – guests mention "no bugs" in reviews. The low cost per window makes it an easy upgrade.

🌍 Global Compatibility & Ordering Process

The Magic Tape Fly Screen works on virtually any window frame material, provided the surface is clean, smooth, and dry (no flaking paint or greasy surfaces).
