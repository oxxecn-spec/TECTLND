Fly Screen: Keep Flies & Large Insects Out

May 2, 2026 5 min read Fly Screen, Fly Mesh

A fly screen is a lightweight mesh barrier designed to block large flying insects such as houseflies, bluebottles, wasps, and moths. It provides strong ventilation and clear visibility while keeping unwanted pests out of your home.

What Is a Fly Screen?

A fly screen uses a wider mesh opening compared to mosquito screens. This design allows maximum airflow while stopping bigger insects. It is ideal for homes where flies are the main problem rather than tiny mosquitoes.

Key Benefits

  • Excellent air circulation and natural light
  • Blocks flies, wasps, bees, and moths
  • Lightweight and easy to install
  • Affordable and long-lasting
  • Suitable for all window and door types

Best Applications

  • Kitchen windows and doors
  • Garage entrances
  • Country homes and farms
  • Cafés and outdoor dining areas
  • Areas with low mosquito activity

Materials

  • PET polyester – durable and flexible
  • Fiberglass – lightweight and cheap
  • Aluminum – strong and rust-resistant

Conclusion

If you want strong ventilation and reliable protection against large insects, a fly screen is the perfect choice. It is simple, cost-effective, and ideal for many US households.
