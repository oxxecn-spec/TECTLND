Top 10 Benefits of Insect Protection Screens for US Homes

May 2, 2026 7 min read Benefits, Insect Screen

Still wondering whether to install insect protection screens on your windows and doors? Here are 10 compelling reasons – backed by health science, energy data, and homeowner experience – to make the switch today.

  1. Prevent mosquito‑borne diseases – West Nile, Zika, and EEE are present in many US states. Screens are your first line of defence.
  2. Better sleep – No buzzing mosquitoes means deeper, uninterrupted rest.
  3. Lower energy bills – Keep windows open instead of running AC; solar screens can reduce cooling costs by 15‑30%.
  4. Pet protection – 304 stainless steel screens resist scratching from dogs and cats, saving you from constant replacements.
  5. Allergy relief – Fine mesh blocks pollen and dust, reducing sneezing and asthma triggers.
  6. Increase home value – Insect screens are a desirable feature for prospective buyers.
  7. No chemical repellents – Physical barrier means no DEET, citronella, or plug‑ins that can irritate lungs.
  8. Rental‑friendly options – Adhesive magnetic nets leave no damage, perfect for apartments.
  9. Child safety – Unlike some blinds, screens have no dangerous cords.
  10. Year‑round usability – Retractable screens can be stored in winter; fixed screens remain in place.

Bonus: UV & Heat Reduction

Solar‑reflective screens block up to 90% of UV rays, protecting your furniture from fading while keeping rooms cooler.
