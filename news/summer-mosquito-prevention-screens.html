Summer Mosquito Prevention: Why Screens Are Your Best Defense

May 2, 2026 6 min read Summer, Prevention

Summer in the US means backyard barbecues, open windows, and ... mosquitoes. Instead of closing up and turning on the AC, or covering yourself with chemical repellents, consider the most effective, chemical‑free solution: mosquito screens.

The Mosquito Problem in Summer

Mosquitoes breed in standing water and become most active at dusk and dawn – exactly when you want to open your windows and doors for evening breezes. Without protection, you either endure the bugs or seal your home shut.

How Screens Provide Natural Ventilation

Well‑fitted screens allow you to keep windows and doors open 24/7. Fine mesh blocks even the smallest biting insects while letting air flow freely. This reduces your reliance on air conditioning, lowering energy bills.

Chemical vs Physical Barriers

  • Citronella candles, sprays, coils, and plug‑ins release chemicals that can irritate lungs, require constant re‑ordering, and are only effective in small areas.
  • Screens are a one‑time investment, scent‑free, and protect your entire home.

Pre‑Summer Checklist

  1. Inspect all existing screens for holes or tears. Repair or replace.
  2. For windows without screens, measure and order custom screens from MBORMC (adhesive, fixed, or retractable).
  3. For sliding patio doors or French doors, consider folding or bi‑parting screens.
  4. Clean screens to ensure maximum airflow.

What If You Rent?

No‑drill magnetic nets or adhesive velcro screens are perfect for renters. They leave no damage and can be removed seasonally.

Solar Screens for Extra Heat Block

Solar‑reflective screens not only block insects but also reflect up to 90% of UV rays, keeping rooms cooler – perfect for afternoon sun.

Conclusion

Don't let mosquitoes ruin your summer. Install mosquito screens now and enjoy fresh air, lower energy bills, and peace of mind. MBORMC makes custom screens for any US window or door.
