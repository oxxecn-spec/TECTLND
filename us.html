About MBORMC

Custom Mosquito Screens for Private Homes, Hotels, Apartments & Construction Projects – Made in Foshan, Delivered from China to the USA

MBORMC has been manufacturing made-to-measure insect screens for over 15 years. We serve individual homeowners in the USA (one-off custom orders) as well as large-scale projects — hotels, apartment complexes, villa developments, and general contractors. Every order is tailored to your exact window or door dimensions, with no minimum quantity for private customers.

We use premium 6063‑T5 aluminum alloy, PET/304 mesh, and offer full OEM/ODM services including custom packaging and logo printing. Factory direct pricing, global logistics, and flexible MOQ. We ship to all US states — from California to New York, Texas to Florida.
